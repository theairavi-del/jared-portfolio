About

I'm a graphic designer and art director based in New York City, specializing in brand identity, visual systems, and art direction. With over 5 years of experience, I've had the privilege of working with startups, agencies, and established brands to create meaningful visual experiences.

My approach combines strategic thinking with bold aesthetics, always aiming to create work that resonates with audiences and stands the test of time. I believe in the power of simplicity and the importance of every detail.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring the city's museums, trying new coffee shops, or working on personal photography projects.
